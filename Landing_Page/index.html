Program View

Diploma in Engineering offered by Global Institute of Engineering and Technology is a comprehensive program designed to provide students with a strong foundation in engineering concepts and practical skills. This course is ideal for individuals seeking to build a career in the technical field or those aiming to pursue higher education in engineering.

The program is structured to ensure a balance between classroom learning and hands-on practical training. Students gain in-depth knowledge in specialized fields such as Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics, and Computer Science Engineering. Conducted by experienced faculty and industry professionals, the course is aligned with modern industry requirements, ensuring that students are job-ready upon completion.

For working professionals or those aiming for lateral entry into engineering careers, this diploma acts as a gateway to upgrade skills and pursue advanced engineering programs like Diploma . With industry-oriented curriculum and practical exposure, the Diploma in Engineering equips students with the tools they need to excel in their chosen field and secure better career opportunities.

Program Highlights

👉 Direct Entry for 12th PCM Students

Students who have completed their 12th grade with PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) are eligible for lateral entry into the Diploma in Engineering program, allowing them to join directly in the second year of the program.

👉 AICTE Compliance

This program follows the guidelines set by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), ensuring a high-quality technical education that meets industry standards.

Simple Study Program for Lateral Entry Students
I. Flexible Class Timings

Classes are scheduled on evenings, weekends, and holidays to accommodate students who wish to pursue education alongside other commitments.

II. Practical Laboratory Training

Lateral entry students participate in on-campus practical lab sessions every Saturday to gain hands-on experience in their chosen engineering field.

III. Advanced Recorded Lessons

Access to recorded lessons from the Learning Management System (LMS) ensures students can revisit missed or challenging concepts at their convenience.

IV. Virtual Labs and Simulations

Remote virtual labs provide real-time engineering simulations, offering 24/7 accessibility for technical experimentation and skill enhancement.

V. Tailored Evaluation Process

Performance evaluations are customized for lateral entry students, including mid-term and end-term exams, practicals, and viva voce to track progress effectively.

VI. Streamlined Examination System

Students are tested through an optimized examination system, ensuring they meet course objectives without overwhelming study schedules.
